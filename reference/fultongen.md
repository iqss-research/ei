# Voter Transitions

Aggregated data from 289 precincts in Fulton County, Georgia. The
variable `t` represents the fraction voting in 1994 and `x` the fraction
in 1992. Beta_b is then the fraction who vote in both elections, and
Beta_w the fraction of nonvoters in 1992 who vote in the midterm
election of 1994.

## Format

A data frame containing 289 observations.
